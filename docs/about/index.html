Just Thinking is a personal archive — a place to keep essays, conversations, drawings, and the kind of unfinished thoughts that usually live in the margins of a notebook.

It is run by one person, on no schedule, with no agenda except attention. There are no ads. There is no algorithm. New things appear when they are ready.

— with care, J.
